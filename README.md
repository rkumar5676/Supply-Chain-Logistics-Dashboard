# 📊 Samsung Supply Chain & Logistics Dashboard | Power BI

> An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze end-to-end Supply Chain & Logistics operations using business intelligence, data visualization, and DAX.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Samsung Supply Chain & Logistics Dashboard** is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution built in **Microsoft Power BI**. It provides an end-to-end view of supply chain operations, enabling decision-makers to monitor supplier performance, inventory health, shipment efficiency, and customer revenue through interactive visualizations.

The dashboard is designed with a modern Samsung-inspired theme and consists of multiple report pages connected through intuitive navigation and interactive filtering.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall supply chain performance
- Track supplier efficiency and lead time
- Analyze inventory levels and stock movement
- Identify shipment delays and logistics bottlenecks
- Monitor customer revenue and profitability
- Improve operational decision-making through real-time insights
