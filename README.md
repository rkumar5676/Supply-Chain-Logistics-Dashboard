## 📌 Business Problem

Logistics management teams had fragmented, siloed reporting — no single consolidated view of supplier performance, inventory health, shipment efficiency, and customer revenue. Manual Excel reports took hours to compile, were error-prone, and provided no drill-down capability for root cause analysis.

## 💡 Solution

Built a **6-page interactive Power BI dashboard** covering the full supply chain lifecycle — from supplier onboarding to customer delivery — consolidating 6 separate manual Excel reports into one real-time BI solution, **saving 8+ hours per week** in reporting effort.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

| Page | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| 🏠 **Executive Overview** | Gross Revenue, Profit Margin, Perfect Order %, KPI summary |
| 🚚 **Supplier Analytics** | Lead time, quality scores, order quantities by supplier |
| 📦 **Inventory & Production** | Stock levels, safety stock, inventory turnover, defect rates |
| 🛤️ **Shipment Analytics** | Delivery %, shipment delays, carrier performance, cost analysis |
| 👥 **Customer Analytics** | Revenue by customer, discount analysis, channel performance |

## 🔍 Key Insights Delivered

1. **Supplier Performance** — Identified top 3 suppliers by quality score and lowest lead time
2. **Inventory Optimization** — Flagged products below safety stock threshold for replenishment
3. **Shipment Delays** — Carrier-wise delay analysis revealed root causes reducing SLA breaches
4. **Customer Revenue** — Channel-wise revenue breakdown enabled targeted discount strategy
5. **Operational Savings** — Consolidated 6 manual Excel reports into 1 real-time dashboard
